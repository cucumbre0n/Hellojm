Virtual Field Trips

Here's a list of even more virtual field trips. You can visit zoos, farms, look at rocket ships, the great wall of China, and even Mars!

Here are all the zoos you can visit from your computer.

The San Diego Zoo

The San Diego Zoo has free live cameras for lots of different animals. You can look at tigers, koalas, penguins, and more!

The Monterey Bay Aquarium

The Monterey Bay Aquarium has live cameras of all sorts of fish and aquatic life. You can find live cameras for jellyfish, sea-turtles, sharks, and even birds!

The Atlanta Zoo Panda Cam

The Atlanta Zoo has live cameras for pandas.

The Houston Zoo

The Houston Zoo has live feeds of many different animals. You can look at giraffes, elephants, rhinos, and more!

The Georgia Aquarium

The Georgia Aquarium has live feeds of more than just fish. You can check out penguins, beluga whales, sea lions, and more!

The Trevor Zoo Red Panda Cam

The Trevor Zoo has a live stream of red pandas, just like you wanted!

Mars!

Here, you can take a virtual tour of Mars using composites of the planet made from pictures taken by the Mars Rovers!

Farm Tours

You can take virtual farm tours and see where lots of different kinds of foods come from using this link!

US Space & Rocket Center

This video shows a virtual tour of the US Space & Rocket Center in Huntsville, Alabama.

The Great Wall of China

Here's a virtual tour of the Great Wall of China. You can see many different parts of the wall during the summer and winter!

The Boston Children's Museum

Last on the list of things I found, but not least, is another museum, but this one is extra special. It's the Boston Children's Museum! You can explore three whole floors of the museum, and like the rest of the links on this page, it's all for free. There's so many different things to see, and it's a whole museum just for kids!
